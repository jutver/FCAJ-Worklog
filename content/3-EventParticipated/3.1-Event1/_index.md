# Summary Report: “AWS RE:INVENT 2025 RECAP (VIETNAM)”

### Event Details
- **Date & Time:** January 27, 2026
- **Location:** AWS Vietnam Office (Levels 26 & 36), Ho Chi Minh City
- **Participant Role:** Attendee (First Cloud Journey Intern)

### Event Objectives

- Catch up on the major product releases and news from AWS re:Invent 2025 in Las Vegas.
- Explore Generative AI in depth, with a special focus on Amazon Bedrock and Agentic AI.
- Discover recent enhancements in data storage and infrastructure management, highlighting S3 and SageMaker.
- Connect and interact with local tech professionals and AWS Solution Architects.

### Speakers

- **Mr. Thi** – Solution Architect (Focus: Agents and Generative AI)
- **Mr. Tung** – Speaker (Focus: Agentic Search and OpenSearch)
- **Multiple Account Managers and AWS Solution Architects**

### Key Highlights

#### Session 1: Generative AI & Agents
- **Amazon Nova Models**: Overview of the latest powerful foundation models.
- **Bedrock Agents**: Detailed examination of Flows, Orchestration, and the newly introduced Guardrails and Memory features.
- **Agentic AI**: The transition from basic chatbots to independent agents capable of handling complex, multi-stage processes.

#### Session 2: SageMaker Unified Studio & S3 Updates
- **Unified Studio**: An integrated development environment uniting AI Engineers, Data Scientists, and Data Engineers.
- **S3 Tables**: Built-in S3 support for the Apache Iceberg open table format.
- **S3 Vector**: A novel capability for native S3 vector storage, providing substantial cost savings.

#### Session 3 & 4: Search & Multimodal AI
- **OpenSearch Serverless**: Linking Agentic Memory and the Model Context Protocol (MCP).
- **Nova Multimodal Embeddings**: Transforming images and video content into searchable vectors.
- **Bedrock Data Automation**: Streamlining the extraction of valuable data from multimedia files.

#### Session 5: AI Infrastructure
- **SageMaker HyperPod**: Sophisticated orchestration for massive GPU arrays.
- **SageMaker MLflow**: Comprehensive end-to-end tracking and management for ML initiatives.

### Key Takeaways

#### The Future is Agentic
- **Autonomous Workflows**: The evolution from crafting prompts to engineering agents. Context-aware agents can now execute intricate tasks without continuous human oversight.
- **Guardrails are essential**: The rise of autonomous agents necessitates rigorous security boundaries and strict policies.

#### Data & Compute Optimization
- **Cost Efficiency**: S3 Vector drastically alters the landscape for budget-conscious projects relying on vector searches, such as RAG applications.
- **Collaboration**: SageMaker Unified Studio simplifies the transition from data prep to model training.

### Applying to Work

- **Project Integration (Security Platform)**:
    - Assess **Bedrock Agents** for automating vulnerability scans (e.g., utilizing an agent to execute scans, parse logs, and generate reports).
- Deploy **S3 Vector** to cost-effectively maintain vulnerability signatures and logs within the backend infrastructure.
- **Architecture Refinement**: Evaluate the integration of **Cognito** for handling user authentication, inspired by conversations with Solution Architects.
- **Best Practices**: Embrace the "Serverless first" philosophy picked up during the networking discussions.

### Event Experience

Participating in the **“AWS re:Invent 2025 Recap”** at the AWS Vietnam headquarters marked a significant milestone in my internship journey. Standout moments included:

#### Gaining Vision
- The presentations made it obvious that **Agentic AI** represents the next big wave. Observing the Flow Agent demonstration sparked new concepts for our project's reporting module.
- Comprehending **Multimodal RAG** revealed potential use cases beyond mere text, which will be crucial for later stages of our Security Platform involving screenshot evaluations.

#### Technical Validation
- The unveiling of **S3 Vector** confirmed our team's requirement for an economical storage method dedicated to log parsing.
- Engaging with **Mr. Thi** and various Solution Architects cleared up confusion regarding **Serverless** standards and **IAM Policies** that challenged me during Week 3.

#### Community & Networking
- The environment at the AWS Office was vibrant, filled with developers and engineers exchanging practical problem-solving strategies.
- I leveraged the chance to converse about **“TheBois” team project** with seasoned professionals, gaining critical insights into our architectural design.
